# Pricing & Plans

This document outlines the current subscription plans and add-on pricing for the Law Flow application.

## Subscription Plans

All paid plans include a discount when billed annually (2 months free).

| Plan | Monthly Price | Yearly Price | Users | Storage | Cases | Templates | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Trial** | Free | - | 2 | 1 GB | 20 | No | 30-day free trial to explore the platform. |
| **Starter** | $30.00 | $300.00 | 5 | 5 GB | 50 | **Yes** | Perfect for small law firms. |
| **Professional** | $50.00 | $500.00 | 10 | 10 GB | 150 | **Yes** | For growing law firms with more needs. |
| **Enterprise** | $120.00 | $1,200.00 | 15 | 20 GB | 500 | **Yes** | For large firms with high-volume needs. |

## Add-Ons

Add-ons allow you to expand the limits of your current plan without upgrading to a higher tier.

| Add-On | Type | Benefit | Price | Billing |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Extra Users Pack** | Recurring | +5 Users | $15.00/mo | Monthly |
| **Extra Storage Pack** | Recurring | +5 GB Storage | $10.00/mo | Monthly |
| **Extra Cases Pack** | Recurring | +50 Cases | $10.00/mo | Monthly |
| **Document Templates** | One-time | Unlock Templates | $20.00 | One-time payment |

## Notes

*   **Currency**: All prices are in USD.
*   **Annual Billing**: Yearly plans provide equivalent of 2 months free compared to monthly billing.
*   **Add-on Validity**: Recurring add-ons renew automatically each month until cancelled. One-time add-ons (like Document Templates) are permanent purchases.
*   **Trial Expiry**: The Trial plan is valid for 30 days. After this period, the account must be upgraded to a paid plan to continue full access.
